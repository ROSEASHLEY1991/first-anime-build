The Best Of Animes

-Stealing The Spotlight-

Anime often features complex and engaging storytelling with intricate plots, character development, and world-building. It's not uncommon for anime to explore philosophical, psychological, or societal themes. It is known for its distinct and diverse art styles. Characters typically have large, expressive eyes, vibrant colors, and exaggerated facial expressions. The art style can vary significantly, ranging from highly detailed and realistic to more simplified and stylized.


Demon Slayer

Top of the charts

This anime gained massive popularity for its stunning animation and compelling story. The Mugen Train movie, released in 2020, also contributed to its success. "Demon Slayer" explores themes of family, determination, and the struggle between humanity and demonic forces. The series also delves into the backstories and motivations of the characters, adding depth to the narrative.

Jujustu Kasien

Best up coming

Known for its intense and well-animated fight scenes, Jujutsu Kaisen became a hit among anime fans. "Jujutsu Kaisen" explores themes of fear, courage, and the consequences of dealing with supernatural forces. The series delves into the concepts of curses, sorcery, and the moral dilemmas faced by its characters.

My Hero Academia

A Super Good Time

This superhero-themed anime continued to be popular, especially with its ongoing seasons. Both the manga and anime have received widespread acclaim for their compelling characters, well-executed action sequences, and exploration of superhero tropes. "My Hero Academia" has gained a massive fanbase and has become one of the most popular and influential contemporary anime and manga series.

Spy Family

Light Hearted & Fun

The series falls under the genres of action, comedy, and espionage, one of the most popular shows online. The series has received positive reviews for its compelling characters, clever plot twists, and the balance of action and humor. Fans have praised Tatsuya Endo's art style and storytelling, making "Spy x Family" one of the noteworthy titles in the manga landscape.

Attack On Titan

Bloody Mess

The final season of this highly acclaimed series was generating a lot of buzz. It's known for its intricate plot and intense action. "Attack on Titan" received critical acclaim for its intense storytelling, unpredictable plot twists, and well-developed characters. It achieved commercial success both in Japan and internationally.
